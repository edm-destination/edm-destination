How I have to send my FILES?

For MIXING, You export every single stem in full length of the song. To do so solo your mixer channel for that instrument and render the song in full length. Do that with every mixer channel you used along with an understandable name!

NO EFFECTS ON IT (no EQ, no Compressor, no Stereo Imager, no Limiter) 44100 hz, 24 bit, .wav file only (no mp3)

For MASTERING, you need to send the full mixed song in 44100 hz, 24 bit, .wav file. Make sure the file should have a headroom of 6db (the voloume of the loudest part of the song should not exceed -6 db) after mixing.
